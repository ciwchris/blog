About me #

I'm a dog lover and adopted a dog which joins me as I explore the Pacific Northwest. I enjoy getting away on nice country summer motorcycle rides. I've had a longstanding interest in flying since childhood. I took lessons one summer but did not continue. I haven't missed an episode of Survivor since beginning watching halfway through season 3. I've applied twice.

I have the desire to be a continual Learner. I spend too much time listening to podcasts. But they are informative and keep the mind going while doing others things. Although I miss allowing my mind to wander. I enjoy Reading well written informative books on a variety of topics.

I strive to make lifelong healthy decisions. I was a runner until an injury. The feeling of the ground under my feet and the rush of endorphins afterward was magical. Now I volunteer at track events.

I'm a professional software developer. I work in .Net and follow Elixir. I continue to learn about faith and what it means in life. I married later in life and am Learning to be a nourishing Husband.
